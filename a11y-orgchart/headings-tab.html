The Infrastructure, Cloud Architecture, and Assurance division helps to maintain the technology that supports our daily operations. This division contains five functional areas: Facilities and Data Center Services, Linux and Networks, Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Planning, Enterprise Systems Assurance, and Cloud Architecture.

Technical Application Management (TAM) is responsible for the maintenance and support of the University of Illinois enterprise systems and applications. TAM provides access to these systems, service desk support, issue investigation and resolution, analysis and expertise for work requests and ITPC projects, and assistance to university clients.

Application Administration (AA) provides high quality and reliable information technology systems and services delivered in concert with campus-based computing organizations and other System Office units.

Application Development and Support (ADS) is responsible for the development, integration, and quality assurance of more than 65 systems and the management of more than 235 databases, as well as support, analysis, and expertise for ITPC projects.

The Decision Support (DS) team transforms raw data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making. DS is responsible for scoping, designing, and delivering solutions to business problems using a combination of enterprise data warehouse and data analytics methodologies.
